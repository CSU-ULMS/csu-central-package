Welcome to OneSearch!

OneSearch lets you search for books, articles, media, journals, and more from across the CSU libraries, all in one unified search engine.

Sign-in in order to:

  • Request materials from other CSU libraries
  • View full search results. (Some databases only show results when you're signed in.)
  • Renew your checked out materials
  • Create favorites lists and save your citations
  • Export citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
 Research Guides

Need some help getting started with your research? Wondering which sources are the best to use for your major? Check out our handy Research Guides. These guides point you toward useful resources, provide citation help, offer search tips, and more.
Got questions?

We have answers. Our team of friendly librarians is here to help you through the research process and support you with your information needs.

  • Chat with a Librarian online, anytime, 24/7.
  • Submit our online form and someone will respond within 24 hours, Monday through Friday.
  • Call or stop by the Information/Check-Out Desk (707-664-2375) Monday through Thursday from 11 am to 4 pm.
