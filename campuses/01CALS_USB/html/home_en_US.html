Welcome to OneSearch!

OneSearch is Pfau Library's new all-in-one search platform. Find books, articles, media, journals, and more. Plus, easily search for books from all CSU libraries!

Get the most from OneSearch by signing in:

  • Request materials from other CSU libraries.
  • Renew checked-out items, view due dates or fines.
  • Create favorites lists and save citations.
  • Get complete search results (some results may be available only to subscribers).

Read more about the OneSearch project and changes from our old system.
