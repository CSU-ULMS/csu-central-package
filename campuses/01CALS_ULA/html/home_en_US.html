Welcome to OneSearch

OneSearch features an intuitive, mobile-ready single interface that provides a gateway to a wealth of print, electronic and digital collections, and makes it easy to find, explore, cite, save and share books and media from across the CSU Libraries. OneSearch includes CSU+, a new book and media sharing system for the entire CSU.

Primo also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew Books and Videos
  • Create Favorite Lists
  • Export Citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
  • View Full Search Results
 As the OneSearch is a brand new interface, we would like you to help us enhance it. We welcome your comments, feedback and questions.
