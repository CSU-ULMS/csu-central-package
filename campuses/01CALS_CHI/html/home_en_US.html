Welcome to OneSearch, Meriam Library's all-in-one search!

OneSearch is the new CSU-wide search interface. With OneSearch you can:

  • Search for articles, books, course reserves, and more; all in one!
  • Renew checked out items
  • Export citations
  • Create favorites lists
  • Locate and request items from all 23 California State University campus libraries, or
  • Access items only at Meriam Library
