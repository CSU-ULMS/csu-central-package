How does this work?

OneSearch provides simple searching for books and e-books, videos, articles, digital media, and more.

OneSearch also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew books and videos
  • Create favorites lists
  • Export citations to Endnote, Zotero, EasyBib, and other citation managers
  • Request articles and other items through interlibrary loan
  • Borrow from any of the 23 CSU campuses
  • View full search results
