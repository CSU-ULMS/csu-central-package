You can stop by the Reference Desk when we are open.

Or you can contact us through Email, Phone or Chat.

 Questions? Comments? Let us know what you think!
What can you find through OneSearch?

You can search nearly all of our CSUEB Library Resources through OneSearch, as well as search for and request books from all 23 CSU Campuses with CSU+.

If you can't find something through CSU+, you can request it through InterLibrary Loan.
