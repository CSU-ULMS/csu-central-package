Welcome to the New OneSearch

OneSearch provides simple, one-stop searching for books and e-books, articles, digital media, course reserves and more. It will replace the current OneSearch, and the InvestiGator catalog, on June 27, 2017.

OneSearch also helps you manage your research. Sign-in in order to:

  • Renew books
  • Save results and create favorites lists
  • Export citations to Refworks and Endnote Web
  • View full search results
